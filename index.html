6. Summary & Recommendation

Bottom line: Option 2 appointments are not empty conversions. They complete at a higher rate (30.5% vs 26.0%) across all appointment types and filing statuses. The data supports keeping Option 2 routing to Concierge rather than PS, as these customers — most of whom have not actually completed — benefit from the concierge interaction and convert at meaningful rates. The higher DIWM/DIY completion mix for Option 2 also suggests some callers self-serve after their concierge touchpoint, adding incremental value beyond FS conversions alone.
