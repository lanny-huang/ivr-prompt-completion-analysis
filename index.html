7. Summary & Recommendation

Bottom line (corrected for post-contact only): When properly filtering to only post-contact appointments and completions, Option 1 and Option 2 perform very similarly on appointment handling (~40.6% each) and product completion (24.0% vs 25.5%). The story is more nuanced than the uncorrected data suggested:

• Option 2 appointments are legitimate — they're handled and convert at rates comparable to Option 1
• "Not started" Opt 2 callers are the best converters — 51.5% product completion vs 39.5% for Opt 1, a 12pp lift
• FS dominates completers for both prompts — 93.6% Opt 1, 86.0% Opt 2 complete as Full Service. Some downgrade (Opt 2 has ~8% DIWM vs 4% Opt 1), but the vast majority convert to FS
• Concierge paths (Appt + FS WT) outperform PS on FS conversion — combined FS rate is 14.2% Opt 1, 11.3% Opt 2 vs PS at 6.4% and 1.0%
• Opt 2 PS transfer is the biggest route — 31.6% of Opt 2 go to PS (vs 5.3% Opt 1), but PS completions skew DIY/DIWM with only 1.0% FS rate
• Recommendation: keep Option 2 routing to Concierge as long as possible — every Opt 2 customer that gets an appt (18.6% FS rate) or FS warm transfer (6.1% FS rate) is worth far more than the PS alternative (1.0% FS rate)
