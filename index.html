1. Overall: Appointment Handled vs Product Completion

Both metrics favor Option 2. Appointment handled rate is 42.5% vs 40.0% (+2.5pp), and product completion rate is 30.5% vs 26.0% (+4.5pp). Notably, product start rate is slightly lower for Opt 2 (73.3% vs 78.6%), but those who do engage convert to completion at a higher rate.

6. Summary & Recommendation

Bottom line: Option 2 appointments outperform Option 1 on both appointment handling (42.5% vs 40.0%) and product completion (30.5% vs 26.0%). The appointments being scheduled are fulfilled at healthy rates, and the downstream tax filing conversions are meaningful. The data supports keeping Option 2 routing to Concierge. Additionally, Option 2's higher DIWM/DIY mix suggests some customers self-file after the concierge interaction, adding value beyond FS conversions alone.
